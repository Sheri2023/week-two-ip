AKAN NAME GENERATOR

The AKAN naming style is a Ghanian tradition whereby they name a child according to the day of the week they were born


This Akan name generator takes a user's birthday (year, month and date) and gender information, determines the day of the week on which they were born and derives an Akan name depending on their gender.
